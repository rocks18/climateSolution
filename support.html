Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add new technicians?

Go to Settings > Users > Add New. You can invite them via email. They will receive a link to set up their password and download the mobile app.

Can I integrate with QuickBooks?

Yes! We support native integrations with QuickBooks Online and Xero. Visit the Integrations marketplace in your dashboard to connect.

Is there a limit on the number of jobs?

No. All plans include unlimited job creation and history. We only charge based on the number of active users (technicians and admins).

How does the free trial work?

You get full access to the Professional plan for 14 days. No credit card required. At the end of the trial, you can choose a plan or downgrade to a limited free version.
